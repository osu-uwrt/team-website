2015 - 2016 AUV

This vehicle will compete in the 2016 Annual AUVSI RoboSub competition, an event showcasing the best autonomous underwater vehicles created by collegiate teams across the globe. This will be the first vehicle from Ohio State to ever compete in the competition.

Components

Thrusters

The AUV will be powered by a total of ten Blue Robotics T200 Thrusters, giving it a full six degrees of freedom. If all of the thrusters were engaged at the same time, the system could draw a maximum of 3,500 watts of power!

Cameras

Vision will be provided by a system of four Pointgrey Blackfly 3 Machine Vision cameras. Two in the front will provide stereo range finding while simultaneously analyzing the situation in front. Two more will be mounted upwards and downwards to assist in manipulation of objects on the seafloor and upwards to provide guidance in returning home.

Competiton

RoboSub

The 2015 - 2016 AUV Project will be used to compete in the 2016 Annual AUVSI RoboSub Competition. This competition seeks to garner interest and advance development in the field of underwater autonomous vehicles. Very little of the earth's seafloor has been mapped and explored. The field is ever expanding and this competition seeks to keep future professionals on the forefront of this future development.
